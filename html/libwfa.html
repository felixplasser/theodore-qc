The libwfa library

While TheoDORE focuses on an external analysis of the output of standard quantum chemical programs, an integrated wavefunction analysis environment is provided by the wavefunction analysis library libwfa.

The libwfa library was developed by F. Plasser, M. Wormit, S. A. Bäppler, B. Thomitzni, and A. Dreuw at the University of Heidelberg. Currently libwfa is interfaced to the ADC and TDDFT modules of Q-Chem. Extensions to other parts of Q-Chem and other quantum chemical programs are in progress.

The release of libwfa into the public domain is planned. If you are interested in this project please contact me.
